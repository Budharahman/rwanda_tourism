Destinations

Volcanoes National Park

Home of mountain gorillas

Volcanoes National Park is one of Africa’s most famous wildlife destinations, located in the northwestern part of Rwanda. It is part of the Virunga Mountains and is home to the rare and endangered mountain gorillas. Visitors can experience unforgettable gorilla trekking adventures, guided hikes through volcanic landscapes, and encounters with golden monkeys. The park is known for its mist-covered volcanoes, lush bamboo forests, and rich biodiversity, making it a must-visit destination for wildlife lovers and adventure seekers.

Lake Kivu

Relax and enjoy beautiful views

Lake Kivu is one of Africa’s Great Lakes, located along the border between Rwanda and the Democratic Republic of Congo. It offers peaceful beaches, scenic boat rides, and stunning sunset views over rolling hills. Visitors can enjoy activities such as swimming, kayaking, fishing, and relaxing in lakeside resorts. The calm atmosphere and natural beauty make Lake Kivu a perfect destination for rest, honeymoon trips, and weekend getaways.

Nyungwe Forest

Explore nature and wildlife

Nyungwe Forest National Park is one of the oldest and most biodiverse rainforests in Africa, located in southwestern Rwanda. It is famous for its canopy walkway, chimpanzee trekking, and rich variety of primates, birds, and plants. Visitors can walk through dense tropical forest trails, experience breathtaking views from suspended bridges, and discover rare wildlife in their natural habitat. Nyungwe is ideal for eco-tourism and nature exploration.
